Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Impostor Basketball ("the App") is developed and operated by an independent developer. This policy describes how we handle your information.

Data We Do Not Collect

Impostor Basketball does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game data — including player names entered during setup and scores — is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

Analytics

We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage analytics to help us improve the game. This includes:

  • App opens and screen views
  • Game events (e.g., games started, rounds completed)
  • General device information (OS version, screen size)

No name, email, or device ID is linked to any event. PostHog data is used solely to understand how the app is used and to improve the experience.

In-App Purchases

Impostor Basketball offers in-app purchases through Apple's App Store and Google Play. All purchase transactions are processed by Apple or Google. We do not have access to your payment information. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID — no personal data is shared.

Children's Privacy

Impostor Basketball does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no personal data is collected from any user, the app is safe for all ages.

Third-Party Services

  • PostHog — anonymous usage analytics (PostHog Privacy Policy)
  • RevenueCat — subscription management (RevenueCat Privacy Policy)

Data Retention

Since no personal data is collected, there is no personal data to retain. Anonymous analytics data is retained by PostHog according to their data retention policies.

Disclaimer

Impostor Basketball is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the NBA, any basketball team, player, or governing body. All player names and references are used for informational and entertainment purposes only.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
